# Grill Me

Interview the user about their plan or design until reaching shared understanding.

## Method

Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving one dependency at a time.

For each decision point:
1. **State your understanding** of what the user wants
2. **Ask one question** — only one at a time
3. **Provide your recommendation** with a brief rationale
4. **Wait for user response** before continuing

## What to challenge
- Assumptions the user made without evidence
- Tradeoffs not acknowledged
- Scope decisions (too big, too small, wrong boundary)
- Ordering of steps (are dependencies respected?)
- Missing stakeholders or constraints
- Vague terms that need definition

## What NOT to do
- Don't ask multiple questions at once
- Don't propose solutions prematurely
- Don't skip branches because the answer seems obvious
- Don't assume the user has thought of everything — ask

## Exit
Stop when: the user says "enough", or every branch of the decision tree has been explored and agreed.
